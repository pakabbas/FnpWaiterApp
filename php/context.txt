Foodnpals is a Ubereats like app for instant 
table booking and food ordering while the customer is on the way to restaurant 

it has 2 parts:
1. For users, which is on root
Home.php: Where All Restaurants appears for exploration
Profile.php: When user clicks on a restaurant, this page is opened and user can select table, add food to cart, view gallery
Confirmation.php: When a booking is made, its details are shown here with map, food ordered

Color theme: #4cbb17 and white

2nd Part: in admin folder
This is where restaurant will login to manage Bookings, Menu, Tables, Users, etc
